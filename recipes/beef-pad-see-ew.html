Beef Pad See Ew

Description

With just 15 minutes and 5-ingredients you can be serving this quick and tasty homemade beef pad see ew for dinner tonight.

Ingredients

  • 340g packet thick egg noodles
  • 500g beef rump steak, thinly sliced
  • 600g packet rainbow stir-fry mix
  • 205g packet pad see ew cooking sauce
  • Coriander sprigs

Method

  1. Cook the 340g packet thick egg noodles in a large saucepan of boiling water for 4 mins or until tender. Drain well.

  2. Meanwhile, heat a wok or large non-stick frying pan over high heat. Add half the 500g beef rump steak, thinly sliced and 600g packet rainbow stir-fry mix for 1-2 mins or until just cooked through. Transfer to a plate. Repeat with the remaining beef.

  3. Return the beef to the wok or pan with the stir-fry mix and add 205g packet pad see ew cooking sauce. Stir-fry for 2-3 mins or until the vegetables are tender and the mixture is heated through. Season.

  4. Divide the beef mixture among serving bowls. Season. Sprinkle with chilli flakes from the pad see ew packet and coriander sprigs to serve.

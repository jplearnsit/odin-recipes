Cheesy Meatball Sausage Rolls

Description

A filling snack in under 25 minutes is ideal for hungry hordes. The kids will love this imaginative, delicious spin on meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 4 x 20cm round wraps
  • 12 (240g) small pork & beef meatballs
  • 2 tbsp heated pizza sauce
  • 55g (1/2 cup) grated mozzarella
  • Basil leaves (optional)

Method

  1. Place 3 small pork & beef meatballs close together in a row on each one of the 20cm round wraps, in the centre one-third of the way in from the front edge.

  2. Working one at a time, fold the front edge of the wrap over the meatballs, then fold in the sides and roll up to enclose. Spray all over with oil. Place into 1 well of the sausage roll maker seam-side down (it might not fit neatly).

  3. Close the lid, turn on the sausage roll maker and cook for 10 minutes. Open the lid and spoon 2 tbsp headted pizza sauce over the top of each sub. Sprinkle with 55g (1/2 cup) grated mozzarella. Prop the lid open with the handle of a wooden spoon, to create a gap of about 2cm. Cook for a further 1-2 minutes until the cheese has melted. Season. Serve warm, scattered with basil leaves, if using.
